How To Play

The game will be played by 3 players. Each player will take turns adding one vertical line or one horizontal line to connect two adjacent dots. This game is played with a 4x4 grid of dots. A point is given to the player who completes a 1x1 square. An extra move is rewarded to the player who gets a point. The game ends when every dots have been connected. The player who garnered the most points at the end of the game is the winner.
